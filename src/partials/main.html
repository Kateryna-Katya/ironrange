About the Game

A rotating defense puzzle with arcade precision

Armor: Color Circles Breaker blends arcade reflexes with puzzle strategy.

In front of you spins a wall of circular defenses built from polygonal tiles.
Some tiles shatter instantly when hit, opening a path toward the center. Others are solid barriers that stop your shot completely. It's not just about shooting - you've gotta nail the timing or you're screwed. Every ring rotates at its own rhythm, forcing you to read the movement and choose your timing carefully.

One correct shot can break through multiple layers of defense and reach the protected core. One mistake can send your ball crashing into an unbreakable wall. As levels progress, the rotating patterns become more complex. Rings shift direction, expand, or create unexpected obstacles. What starts as basic timing quickly turns into this crazy dance of watching patterns and waiting for your shot.
